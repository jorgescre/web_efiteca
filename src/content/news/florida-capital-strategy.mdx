## A leaner operating model

This starter keeps the platform intentionally simple:

- static pages by default,
- market-aware routing only when needed,
- and a lightweight edge decision at the homepage entry point.

That combination gives Efiteca a more stable publishing workflow and stronger cache behavior across regions.
